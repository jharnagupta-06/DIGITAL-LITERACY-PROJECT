# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

## ✅ Do’s:
- Think before posting anything online  
- Use respectful and polite language  
- Protect your personal information  
- Verify information before sharing  
- Maintain a professional online presence  

## ❌ Don’ts:
- Do not share fake or misleading information  
- Avoid posting offensive content  
- Do not overshare personal details  
- Avoid unnecessary arguments  
- Do not misuse social media platforms
